French Manicure

The elegant and restrained look of one of the eminent designs has long undergone changes. Now it is made in a wide variety of colors, with rhinestones, and drawings. The ruffian and the “reverse” french are most notably. But many women adhere to the classic version with a natural nail base and a colorless tip.
